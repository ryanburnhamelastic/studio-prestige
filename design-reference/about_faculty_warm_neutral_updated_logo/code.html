Our Legacy

Nurturing movement with grace since 1998.

Studios Prestige was founded on a simple belief: that every child deserves a sanctuary where discipline meets whimsy, and where excellence is cultivated through kindness.

"We don't just teach steps; we teach the courage to take them."

— Miss Jess Thorne

Miss Jess Thorne

Founder Artistic Director
school BFA in Dance, NYU Tisch
verified_user RAD Registered Teacher

Jess Thorne's journey began at the age of four, draped in oversized tutus and fueled by a relentless curiosity for movement. After a professional career spanning stages from Broadway to London's West End, Jess returned to her roots with a vision to redefine dance education.

Recognized for her empathetic approach, Jess has spent the last two decades crafting a curriculum that balances rigorous technical standards with psychological well-being. She believes the studio should be a "nurtured nest"—a safe space where young artists can fail forward, find their voice, and ultimately, fly.

Under her leadership, Studios Prestige has produced dancers who now grace the world's most prestigious conservatories, but more importantly, it has nurtured thousands of confident young people who carry the discipline of dance into every walk of life.
